    FAQ

    How long does a wallet transaction take?

    Does not exceed the time of a standard Ethereum transaction, as a rule, if you have not reached it, check the correctness of the transaction.

    How many prizes are awarded?

    The amount of prizes (hence the total amount of money awarded) changes dynamically.

    How can I claim my prizes?

    If you win, the winning amount will automatically appear in your wallet.

    How are the winners chosen?

    The protocol is decentralized and open source. This means that anyone can review and test the code to make sure it's fair and secure.

    Can you increase your chances of winning?

    Your chances are the same, but due to the larger number of coins on different wallets, the chance to win grows.
